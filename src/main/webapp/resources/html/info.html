Mozu Sterling Order Management application provides integration with IBM Sterling Order Management .

Included with this application are synchronization of:

  • Inventory
  • Orders
  • Customers

Once you set up your Mozu Sterling Order Management application application you'll be on your way to managing the orders from across your company in one place!

To manage your Sterling Order Management account, please visit contact your IBM sales representative.
